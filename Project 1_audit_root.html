FAQ

Questions, answered.

Still stuck? Email us and we'll get back today.

  • Do I need to know how to code?

    Nope. You type a product name and one sentence. Drumroll generates the page and the referral mechanics for you.

  • Why do I bring my own Anthropic API key?

    Two reasons. (1) Your token cost goes straight to Anthropic — typically less than a cent per waitlist — instead of us marking it up 5x like most AI products. (2) Your usage stays in your control: pause it, swap models, switch providers later, no lock-in. The key lives only in your browser; we proxy each call without ever logging it.

  • Can I use it without an API key?

    Yes. Without a key, copy comes from a clean template generator — still hosts a beautiful waitlist, just without the AI polish. Add a key anytime to upgrade existing waitlists.

  • Can I use my own domain?

    Yes — Pro and Team plans support custom domains. Add a CNAME and we handle SSL automatically. The page lives on your domain, not ours.

  • How is this different from Tally / waitlist.email / getwaitlist?

    Those are generic form builders. Drumroll is purpose-built for AI startups — built-in viral referrals, AI-drafted copy in your product's voice, and conversion-tuned page templates that actually look like a 2026 launch.

  • Where is my data stored?

    Turso (managed libSQL) in production, or local SQLite if you self-host. You can export to CSV any time, and Pro+ plans have webhooks + direct integrations with Resend, ConvertKit, and Loops.

  • How do I pay?

    Crypto only — Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT and 300+ other coins via NOWPayments. Sub-1% transaction fees mean more money to the founder, less to payment processors. No cards, no LLC required, no PayPal-style chargebacks.

  • Is there a refund?

    30-day no-questions-asked refund on Pro and Team. We don't want unhappy customers.
